Introducing ReFill

Your Future Snack Solution

What is ReFill?

ReFill is a platform that allows users to choose whatever pantry snacks they want from their local grocery stores and have them shipped to their homes on a customizable subscription schedule. Your snack choices, your schedule.

  • Have regular meetings? Use ReFill to save time from your busy schedule and keep your colleagues' brains stimulated
  • Working hard with a busy schedule at school? Watch for your ReFill shipment and keep on the grind
  • Many diet-affecting diseases require regular consumption of special dietary ingredients. Allow ReFill to provide nutritious snacks without the hassle of going out.
  • Minimal custom CSS so you are free to explore your own unique design options
